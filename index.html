// What We Do

Five Core Services.
Built for Canadian SMBs.

Tiered cybersecurity solutions designed to protect businesses at every stage, delivered remotely or on-site, anywhere in Canada.

01

Managed Security Services (MSSP-Lite)

Enterprise-grade monitoring at SMB prices. We act as your dedicated security team so you never need to hire one full-time.

  • Endpoint Detection and Response (EDR)
  • Firewall monitoring and log management
  • 24/7 alerting basic SOC
  • Monthly reporting and threat hunting

Most SMBs lack full-time IT security teams. A cost-effective MSSP fills this gap.

Industries: Professional Services (Law and Accounting), Construction and Engineering, Real Estate
02

Cybersecurity Risk Assessment and Compliance

Understand your exposure and obligations. We assess your risks and deliver a clear roadmap to compliance and resilience.

  • Risk assessment reports (NIST/CIS aligned)
  • Gap analysis: PIPEDA, HIPAA, PCI-DSS
  • Remediation roadmap
  • Security policy templates

Many SMBs are unaware of their cyber risks or regulatory obligations. This positions you as a strategic advisor.

Industries: Healthcare Clinics and Dentists, Financial Advisors, Retail and E-commerce
03

Cloud Security Configuration Review

Businesses moved to the cloud post-COVID without securing it. We identify every gap and harden your environment wherever you are hosted.

  • Azure, AWS and Google Workspace security review
  • Microsoft 365 hardening (MFA, conditional access)
  • Email security: phishing and spoofing protection
  • Backup and business continuity verification

Businesses moved to the cloud but many lack secure configuration, leaving critical data exposed.

Industries: Startups and SaaS, Non-profits, Education and Training Institutes
04

Security Awareness Training and Phishing Simulations

Your people are your last line of defence. We train them to recognise and resist attacks, the most cost-effective risk mitigation available.

  • Quarterly phishing simulations
  • Employee awareness sessions (virtual or on-site)
  • Acceptable Use and Password Policy creation
  • BYOD policy creation

Human error remains the number 1 threat vector for small businesses. Education is cost-effective risk mitigation.

Industries: Legal and Insurance, Small Manufacturing, Accounting and Tax Firms
05

Incident Response Planning and Retainer

When not if an incident occurs, you need a plan. We build your IRP, run tabletop exercises, and offer an optional monthly retainer.

  • Incident Response Plan (IRP) creation
  • Tabletop exercises (annual or semi-annual)
  • Optional retainer: approx 10 hrs/month breach response

SMBs rarely have IR plans in place. This service provides both preparedness and peace of mind.

Industries: Logistics and Transportation, Healthcare, Energy Subcontractors and Field Services
+

Optional Add-Ons That Scale With You

As your business grows, so does your threat surface. These add-ons layer on top of any core package and are available to all tiers.

  • Vulnerability Scanning-as-a-Service
  • Penetration Testing (annual or ad-hoc)
  • Virtual CISO (vCISO) consulting

Growing businesses need a strategic security advisor without the cost of a full-time CISO. Our vCISO fills that role.

Available to: All service tiers and standalone engagements
